Exceptional Bases

Gear bases that can roll an extra rune socket — the strongest version of every slot. Kept as white items to craft on, and optionally ANY unique that drops on one.

🧱 White bases

Exceptional bases can roll an extra rune socket (e.g. gloves with 2 instead of 1) — that's what makes them special and worth crafting on. Blank (Normal) ones are kept to craft or sell to crafters. No market listing, so your value floors don't apply; the quality and item-level gates below decide what's worth the pickup.

💎 Uniques on these bases

Normally a unique below your value floor is left on the ground. This picks up every unique that drops on one of the bases listed below, price ignored — the exceptional version can roll an extra rune socket, so it's the better copy of that unique and usually worth the slot.
